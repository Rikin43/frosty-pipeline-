// Our Story

Made Slowly. Eaten Quickly.

Scoops & Dreams started in a tiny kitchen in Ahmedabad in 1994. Our founder, Meena Aunty, believed ice cream wasn't a dessert — it was a mood. Thirty years later, we still churn every batch by hand using her original recipes.

We source our cream from local dairies, our fruits from Gujarat's farmers markets, and our nuts directly from growers in Kashmir. No preservatives. No compromises.
